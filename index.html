About Company

GEM PRIVATE TUTOR was established in 2024 to provide high-quality, personalized tutoring services both online and onsite. Our goal is to bring exceptional education to students' homes, catering to a broad array of subjects, including Mathematics, English, Biology, Business Studies, Chemistry, Physics, Computer Science, Chinese Language, etc. At GEM, we pride ourselves on offering tailored education, ensuring that each student's unique needs are met.

Vision

To empower students with the highest standard of education, customized to their learning needs.
To empower teachers across Indonesia by providing opportunities for additional income through private tuition services.
